Mysterious places in india

Magnetic Hill, Ladakh: A natural wonder that appears to defy gravity, causing vehicles to roll uphill when left in neutral. Scientific investigations have revealed that this is an optical illusion caused by the road's construction and the surrounding slopes.
Kuldhara, Rajasthan: An abandoned village that was once a prosperous settlement. Local legends say that the Paliwal Brahmins who lived there cursed the village to be haunted to keep people away.
Karni Mata Temple, Rajasthan: Also known as the temple of rats, this temple is home to over 20,000 rats that worship along with the goddess Karni Mata.
Jatinga, Assam: A mysterious village known for its unusual bird suicides.
Dow Hill Forest, West Bengal: A wooded region that is said to be flooded with paranormal mysteries and the site of many murders.
Bhangarh Fort, Rajasthan: Known as one of the most haunted places in India and Asia.
